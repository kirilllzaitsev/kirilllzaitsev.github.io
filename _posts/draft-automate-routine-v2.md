## Tools to speed-up your programming routines

idea: programmer may choose to waste time by pressing GUI buttons each time he deploys a new change or
automate the process and use a keyboard shortcut for that. This effect accumulates and becomes one of the drivers for either low or high performance.

sides of an issue
    more time
    distraction from creative process
    annoyance by repeating the same thing
    a place for natural human-based mistakes (misclicks, typos, etc.)

the moral
    optimize routine execution with tools
    still, requires to select a suitable tool

conclusion
    Optimization of routine workflows should receive a proper consideration by everyone who wants to be efficient at their work. By repeating the same sequence of manual actions over and over again, developers spend more time than needed for the job, instead of tackling challenges for their creativity and skill. Moreover, manual work is naturally more error-prone than automated. The final message is the following: don't spend your time doing repetitive things! Focus on the creative part of your job, even if at the moment it means to figure out how to automate your programming routines.
